Reports of the Indian skipper crossing 200 kmph while driving his car
on his way to Pune's Maharashtra Cricket Association Stadium emerged on

the Mumbai-Pune Expressway
and the fine was generated in the automatic speeding system for crossing the set 105 kmph limit.
